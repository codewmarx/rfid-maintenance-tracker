Marko Garado and Groupmates

Our RFID system, designed in completion for Sir Archie Ranido, is the brainchild of Marko Garado and his dedicated groupmates. This innovative system represents a pivotal advancement in equipment management, offering a seamless solution for logging and tracking valuable assets. At its core, our RFID system aims to revolutionize how organizations manage their equipment, ensuring streamlined operations and enhanced efficiency. By integrating cutting-edge RFID technology, we have created a system that not only simplifies equipment tracking but also provides invaluable insights for better decision-making. Our passion for innovation and commitment to excellence drive us to continually enhance our system, ensuring it remains at the forefront of asset management solutions.
